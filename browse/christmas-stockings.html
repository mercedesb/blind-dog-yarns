Made to order Christmas stockings

My stockings feature a variety of whimsical characters and Christmas themed pictures stitched into them. Finished stockings measure approximately 8in across and 18in from top to toe. The stockings are large enough to be filled with stocking stuffers but also perfect if you're just looking to add some Christmas cheer to your holiday decor.

Each stocking takes approximately 12-15 hours to complete and all are made to order. This allows you to customize your color choices, which direction the stocking faces, and if you want personalization.

My stockings are made from 100% acrylic yarn. Acrylic yarn holds up better to washing and drying and being stuffed full of gifts than wool or other natural fibers. Machine wash delicate, tumble dry low. All items made in a smoke-free, dog friendly home.
